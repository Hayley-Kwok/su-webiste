Welcome to the homepage of Students' Union which mainly designed for students who are computer science undergraduates. This page is NOT an official website for Student Union Building, it is created by a Computer Science undergraduate.

Students' Union is located in the Western Bank, which is just 5 minutes walk from the Diamond, 15 minutes walk from city centre. It was built in 2009 and completed in 2010. There are wide varieties of food and services in this building.
