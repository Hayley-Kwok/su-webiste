Different Areas in the Building

Teaching Space

There is a multi-purpose area called Auditorium that can be used by lecturers and students for lectures, or a place for drama or some indoor sports.


Support Service

SSID is one of the famous service for the students, students in University of Sheffield can seek help in SSID, whatever problems like support and guidance, healthcare and well-being or even mental-wellbeing advices.
The Website of SSID


Retail outlet

The retail called "Our Shop" on level 3 is one of the attractions in Students’ Union as it stored wide varieties of items around the world especially food which normal store doesn’t have. For example, Chinese lemon tea, soymilk, or noodle from Asia. One of the famous products is The University notebook which is with cheap price and good quality.
The Website of Our Shop


Sheffield Students' Union Store

Sheffield Students' Union Store is a shop which sell different exclusive products like hoodies and jackets.
