Restaurants

Coffee Revolution

You should try the freshest coffee with breakfast or hot chocolate with sandwiches as lunch. The all-day breakfast there is wonderful!
The Website of Coffee Revolution


Bar One

You can try to order the handmade burger in Bar One which the juicy beef burger is absolutely brilliant with crispy chips. I would suggest the double cheeseburger with lettuce and tomato pair with freshly fried potato chips.
The Website of Bar One


Interval

If burger is not your cup of tea, pizza from Interval is probably an alternative choice, there are many toppings available to add on the pizza, you can sit down and share the big pizza together.
The Website of Interval


New leaf

If pizza is too greasy for you, or if you are vegetarian, New leaf is probably a healthy and refreshed choice, there are many healthy salads or wraps to select.
The Website of New Leaf
