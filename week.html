Typical week


Students’ Union is one of the best designed building in Sheffield. For computer Science students, the building can bring out quite a lot of help and fun to them.

A typical week of students may start with a breakfast from Our Store. There is a meal deal with a drink, a sandwich and a snack for 3 pounds.

There are also many different activities, services and events held in the building.

For events, each week there are clubbing events in this Building’s Foundry, it really makes your stress away with appropriate amount of alcohol and free dancing.
The Website of Foundry Sheffield

Besides, there are always different kind of special events happening there like Societies Fair and Housing Fair which provide a lot help to students. For more information, just go on to the website of the Students' Union to find your interested activities.
The Website of Students' Union

On the other hand, there are many interesting activities happening in the building. For example, different give it a go activities. Some of them require tickets which you can buy it from the box office in the building. You can have more information of those activities by visiting the website below.
Information of different events

The building also consists of different places that students can make a good use of.

For example, Uni central that have different type of food stores from burgers to pizza.

Moreover, at the forth floor of the building, there are plenty of spaces which students can study there with their friends with less restriction than the libraries. That is a pretty good space for group discussion which you can enjoy food and don’t need to worry about disturbing others during your discussion.
